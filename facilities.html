Laboratories

Our state-of-the-art laboratories provide students with hands-on experience using modern equipment and technologies.

Pharmaceutics Laboratory

Equipped with tablet compression machines, coating pans, dissolution apparatus, and other formulation development equipment.

  • Tablet compression machines
  • Dissolution test apparatus
  • Coating and granulation equipment
Pharmaceutical Chemistry Lab

Features advanced instrumentation for drug synthesis, purification, and analysis including HPLC, GC, and spectrophotometers.

  • HPLC and GC systems
  • UV-Vis spectrophotometers
  • FTIR and NMR spectroscopy
Pharmacology Laboratory

Equipped for in-vitro and in-vivo drug testing, including organ bath systems, ECG machines, and behavioral testing apparatus.

  • Organ bath systems
  • ECG and blood pressure monitors
  • Behavioral testing equipment

Library & Learning Resources

Our comprehensive library provides access to extensive collections of books, journals, and digital resources.

Central Library

Our central library houses over 50,000 volumes, 200+ print journals, and provides access to numerous online databases and e-journals.

  • 50,000+ books and references
  • 200+ print journals
  • Online database access
  • Digital library with e-books
  • Quiet study areas and group discussion rooms
Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with 100+ systems, high-speed internet, and specialized pharmaceutical software.

Wi-Fi Campus

High-speed wireless internet connectivity across the entire campus for students and faculty.

Smart Classrooms

Technology-enabled classrooms with audio-visual equipment and interactive learning tools.

Auditorium

500-seat auditorium with advanced audio-visual systems for seminars, conferences, and events.
