Academic Departments

Our departments are at the forefront of pharmaceutical education and research, each specializing in different aspects of pharmaceutical sciences.

Department of Pharmaceutics

The Department of Pharmaceutics focuses on the design, development, and evaluation of drug delivery systems. Our faculty and students work on innovative formulations to improve drug efficacy and patient compliance.

Key research areas include:

  • Novel drug delivery systems
  • Nanotechnology in drug delivery
  • Controlled release formulations
  • Biopharmaceutics and pharmacokinetics
  • Industrial pharmacy and technology transfer
Learn More

Department of Pharmacology

The Department of Pharmacology explores how drugs interact with biological systems. Our research spans from molecular mechanisms to clinical applications, with a focus on developing safer and more effective therapeutics.

Key research areas include:

  • Molecular pharmacology and drug targets
  • Cardiovascular and neuropharmacology
  • Cancer pharmacology
  • Toxicology and safety pharmacology
  • Clinical pharmacology and pharmacogenomics
Learn More

Department of Pharmaceutical Chemistry

The Department of Pharmaceutical Chemistry focuses on the design, synthesis, and analysis of drug molecules. We combine traditional medicinal chemistry with modern computational approaches to develop new therapeutic agents.

Key research areas include:

  • Medicinal chemistry and drug design
  • Computer-aided drug design
  • Natural product chemistry
  • Analytical method development
  • Quality control and assurance
Learn More

Department of Pharmacognosy

The Department of Pharmacognosy explores medicinal resources from nature. Our research focuses on the discovery, development, and standardization of natural products for therapeutic applications.

Key research areas include:

  • Medicinal plant research
  • Natural product isolation and characterization
  • Phytochemistry and standardization
  • Ethnopharmacology and traditional medicine
  • Marine and microbial natural products
Learn More

Department of Pharmacy Practice

The Department of Pharmacy Practice focuses on the clinical aspects of pharmacy, patient care, and healthcare outcomes. We prepare students for roles in clinical pharmacy, hospital practice, and community pharmacy.

Key research areas include:

  • Clinical pharmacy and therapeutics
  • Pharmacoepidemiology and outcomes research
  • Pharmacy management and economics
  • Patient counseling and education
  • Interprofessional healthcare collaboration
Learn More
