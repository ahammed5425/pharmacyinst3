Admission Requirements

For B.Pharm Program
  • Completed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in qualifying examination
  • Valid score in PCET or State-level Pharmacy Entrance Examination
For M.Pharm Program
  • B.Pharm degree from a recognized institution
  • Minimum 55% aggregate marks in B.Pharm
  • Valid GPAT score or PCET score for M.Pharm
For Pharm.D Program
  • Completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in qualifying examination
  • Valid score in PCET or equivalent entrance examination
