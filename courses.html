Undergraduate Programs

Our undergraduate programs provide a strong foundation in pharmaceutical sciences and prepare students for diverse career paths in healthcare.

UG
Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

A comprehensive 4-year program that provides fundamental knowledge in pharmaceutical sciences and prepares students for careers in pharmacy practice, industry, and research.

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Science
  • Intake: 100 Students
Learn More
UG
B.Pharm (Pharmaceutical Chemistry)

Specialized program focusing on drug design, synthesis, and analysis. Students learn about medicinal chemistry, analytical techniques, and quality control.

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Science
  • Intake: 60 Students
Learn More
UG
B.Pharm (Pharmacology)

Focuses on drug actions, mechanisms, and therapeutic uses. Students study pharmacokinetics, toxicology, and clinical pharmacology.

  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Science
  • Intake: 60 Students
Learn More

Postgraduate Programs

Our postgraduate programs offer specialized knowledge and research opportunities for advanced careers in pharmaceutical sciences.

PG
Master of Pharmacy (M.Pharm)

A 2-year postgraduate program offering specialization in various pharmaceutical disciplines. Students engage in advanced coursework and research projects.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: B.Pharm
  • Intake: 30 Students
Learn More
PG
M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics)

Specialization in drug formulation, development, and delivery systems. Focus on novel drug delivery, biopharmaceutics, and industrial pharmacy.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: B.Pharm
  • Intake: 20 Students
Learn More
PG
M.Pharm (Pharmaceutical Chemistry)

Advanced study of drug design, synthesis, and analysis. Includes computational chemistry, spectroscopy, and pharmaceutical analysis.

  • Duration: 2 Years
  • Eligibility: B.Pharm
  • Intake: 20 Students
Learn More

Doctoral & Professional Programs

Our doctoral and professional programs prepare students for leadership roles in pharmacy practice, research, and academia.

Doctoral
Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D)

A 6-year professional doctoral program focusing on clinical pharmacy and patient care. Includes extensive clinical rotations and training.

  • Duration: 6 Years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Science
  • Intake: 30 Students
Learn More
Doctoral
Ph.D. in Pharmaceutical Sciences

Research-intensive doctoral program for students interested in academic or industrial research careers. Specializations available in various pharmaceutical disciplines.

  • Duration: 3-5 Years
  • Eligibility: M.Pharm/M.Sc
  • Intake: 15 Students
Learn More
PG Diploma
PG Diploma in Clinical Research

One-year program focusing on clinical trial design, management, and regulatory affairs. Prepares students for careers in clinical research organizations.

  • Duration: 1 Year
  • Eligibility: B.Pharm/MBBS/BDS
  • Intake: 25 Students
Learn More

Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to identify talented and motivated students who will thrive in our academic environment.

1
Application Submission

Submit online application with required documents before the deadline.

2
Entrance Examination

Appear for the PharmaCare Entrance Test (PCET) or other accepted exams.

3
Interview & Counseling

Shortlisted candidates will be called for interview and counseling session.

4
Admission Offer

Successful candidates will receive admission offers based on merit.
