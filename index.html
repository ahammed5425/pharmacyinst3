About PharmaCare University

Established in 1985, PharmaCare University has been at the forefront of pharmaceutical education and research in India. Our institution is committed to developing skilled professionals who can meet the evolving healthcare needs of society.

We offer a comprehensive range of programs from diploma to doctoral levels, all designed to provide students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

Research & Innovation

Our faculty and students are engaged in cutting-edge research that addresses critical challenges in pharmaceutical sciences and healthcare.

Drug Discovery

Developing novel therapeutic agents for various diseases including cancer, diabetes, and infectious diseases.

Formulation Development

Creating advanced drug delivery systems for improved therapeutic efficacy and patient compliance.

Pharmacogenomics

Studying how genes affect a person's response to drugs for personalized medicine approaches.
